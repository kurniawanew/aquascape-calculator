*Nilai Lumen LED 1W

**Kategori Pencahayaan Berdasarkan Lumen/Liter
  • 15 - 25 lumen/liter = Low Light / Easy
  • 25 - 50 lumen/liter = Medium Light
  • > 50 lumen/liter = High Light / Advance
